* Utility

## Protect ##

* Advanced
* Reaction
* Utility
* Provides complete immunity to an attack. The second time a pokemon uses Protect in a battle, it has a 4 in 8 chance of failure, 6 in 8 for a third use, and 7 in 8 for a fourth or higher use. The failure rate resets to 0 after a failed use.
